Hi! I'm Stephen Reyes, a certified Amazon Virtual Assistant with a strong foundation built across 12+ years of professional experience in the construction and e-commerce industries.

I began my career as a Purchaser in the construction industry (2013–2021), where I developed sharp skills in procurement, vendor coordination, budgeting, and attention to detail. In late 2021, I transitioned into Amazon e-commerce as a Product Researcher, gaining direct hands-on experience with Seller Central and product discovery tools.

Since 2022, I've been working as a Document Controller, further strengthening my skills in organized reporting, process management, and meticulous documentation — all directly transferable to running a professional Amazon VA service.
